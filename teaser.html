Your homework
Before you show up
  • Watch the films. The more you know, the better you draft. No shame in trailer-only prep, but you will feel it in the picks.
  • Know the nominees. All 24 categories are in play. Best Picture, Director, all the acting categories, and the technical awards too.
  • Have opinions. You will need to assign a confidence value to every single category. Gut feeling counts.
  • No spoilers. We're watching live together. Don't read the predictions obsessively if you want the night to feel real.
